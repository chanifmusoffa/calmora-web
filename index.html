Introduction

What is CALMORA?

CALMORA is an innovative herbal tea made from a combination of corn silk, bay leaves, and cinnamon—natural ingredients rich in bioactive compounds that offer a range of potential health benefits. Corn silk, which has long been considered agricultural waste, is repurposed as the main ingredient, thereby adding value while supporting the concept of sustainability. Combined with bay leaves and cinnamon known for their high antioxidant content, CALMORA offers a practical, natural, and eco-friendly alternative for a healthy beverage.

Amid growing public awareness of healthy lifestyles and the consumption of natural products, CALMORA offers a solution in the form of a herbal beverage that not only has a distinctive taste and soothing aroma but also contributes to reducing agricultural waste. By championing the concepts of health and sustainability, CALMORA is expected to become an innovative product that benefits consumers while creating economic value through the utilization of local resources.
